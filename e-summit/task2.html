VIRTUAL IDEATHON

An ideathon is a short,intensive,brainstorming event to help young talents generate fresh solutions to
existing challenges in their communities.Participants work in teams and use innovate ideation practices
such as design thinking to brainstorm on possible solutions

RULES

2-4 members
Topic for Ideathon can be chosen from any domain/ field.

Abstract Format:
1.Problem
2.Solution
3.Approach
